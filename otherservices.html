STAY AND PLAY AT AL-RAHMA LODGE AND RESTAURANT

VARIETY OF REACREATIONAL ACTIVITIES

In today's world, it's becoming increasingly important for hoteliers to offer something more than a luxury stay for their guests.

AL-Rahma Lodge offers our esteemed guests a variety of recreational activities to choose from. These include swimming, football, volleyball, netball, cycling, boating, lawn racket ball, dart, many indoor games, guided nature walk and the Rahma trail, which gives you the opportunity to blow off steam during your stay.


For our little friends, we have a well-equipped kids' corner and a large colorful Bouncing castle to keep them engaged as you enjoy your stay with us.
