CONFERENCE EVENTS IN KENYA

AL-Rahma Lodge offers unique hotel conference facilities and events venues in Garissa, from seminars to conferences and accommodation at our exclusive lodge in Garissa. With a variety of extra services to help you plan and organize a successful conference or workshop, we have 8 Conference halls, 2 boardrooms with breakaway rooms on request, as well as large spacious grounds for outdoor conferences on request.

WHAT WE OFFER OUR ESTEEMED CLIENTS

Our meeting rooms are flooded with natural light, featuring beautiful views over the garden and pool area. Our conference centre, located on the shores of River Tana, feature meeting rooms flooded with natural light, overlooking the garden and pool area. These are the perfect facilities for taking your meeting far away from the noise and distractions of the busy city. Less than two minutes away from the bustling Town, the centre offers complimentary parking on-site-for participants who might not be staying at our lodge, increasing the convenience of your stay.
